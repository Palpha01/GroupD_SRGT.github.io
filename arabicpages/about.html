About Shah Rafiq General Trading LLC.

We here, a general trading company, specializes in exporting spare parts of vehicles such as seacraft vessels, farming equipment, power generators, and heavy-duty transportation.

We deliver the utmost products from multiple brands and sell them to our clients from different countries from the Emirates to as far as Africa to Central Asia and Russia.

That’s why we are dedicated to serve wholesalers with the right product with the right price.

Founded by Pakistani businessman Muzammil Shah in 2009, our mission is to export our products worldwide and help name the right product at the right price without any compromise in quality.

15 years of service and we are still a vibrant small business with a steadily growing economy, while yet have the chance to introduce our international presence to the world.
